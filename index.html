— Who I Am

A storyteller who edits with emotion, not just cuts.

I believe the soul of a great video lives in timing, rhythm, and restraint. Every transition, every pause, and every frame should guide the viewer into a feeling, not just a sequence.

From cinematic color grading to narrative pacing, I craft edits that reflect brand identity while building genuine audience connection. The goal is always the same: make people feel something real.
